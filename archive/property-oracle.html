How this tool works, in 30 seconds

Every property lands in one of four quadrants based on two numbers: how much the client's team uses the product (user adoption) and how many leads the product converts (conversion rate). Drag either slider on the left to redraw the quadrant lines. Counts, colors, and categories all update live.

Click any dot on the scatter plot or any row in the case table to open that property. You'll see the full playbook for its category, a pre-drafted email (or internal ticket for stuck accounts) written to match the situation, and one-click buttons to open the draft in your mail client or copy it to your clipboard. Edit it, send it, come back and mark the case done.

For the full definitions of every category, status, and team role, open the reference guide in the top right.

Thresholds iThe two sliders redraw the quadrant dividers on the scatter plot. Moving them changes which properties belong to which category in real time. Try dragging user adoption to 60 and watch sapphire shores mansion flip from reference to stuck.

User adoption iThe share of the client's leasing team actively using the AI leasing platform week over week. Low adoption means the tool is installed but the humans are not touching it. 30%
Conversion rate iThe share of AI-handled leads that actually turn into tours, applications, or leases. Low conversion means the AI is running but the output is not materializing into revenue. 20%

Sliders divide the portfolio into four quadrants. Each quadrant maps to a category, a default owner, and a playbook.

Portfolio map iEvery dot is one property. Position shows adoption on the x-axis and conversion on the y-axis. Color shows category. Dashed lines are your current thresholds. Click any dot to open that property in the action panel below.

Property UA CR Category iAutomatically computed from the two threshold sliders. Category determines the default owner, the suggested action, and the playbook. Suggested action iThe first move the default owner should make. Full playbook appears in the action panel when you click a row. Owner iStarts as the default owner for that category. Change it inline if someone else is taking the case. Status
